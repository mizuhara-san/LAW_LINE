Media & Intellectual Property

Simple explanations of copyright, trademark, defamation, and digital rights.

Copyright Infringement

Protects original works.

  • Send cease & desist notice.
  • File civil suit for injunction/damages.
  • Register work for stronger protection.

Reference: Copyright Act, 1957

Defamation Complaint

False statement harming reputation.

  • Send legal notice first.
  • File criminal (Section 499 IPC) or civil suit.
  • Online content: approach platform or court.

Reference: Sections 499-500 IPC

Trademark Registration

Protects brand names/logos.

  • Apply online at ipindia.gov.in.
  • Search for existing marks first.
  • Opposition period after publication.

Reference: Trademarks Act, 1999
