Legal Resources for Senior Citizens

Maintenance, welfare, and protection rights for elderly.

Maintenance from Children

Senior citizens can claim maintenance.

  • File application with Maintenance Tribunal.
  • Monthly allowance up to ₹10,000.
  • Tribunal decision within 90 days.

Reference: Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act, 2007

Protection from Abuse

Right to live with dignity.

  • Report abuse to Tribunal or police.
  • Eviction of abusive children from property.
  • Old age homes as option.

Reference: Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act, 2007

Healthcare Benefits

Priority in treatment and schemes.

  • Separate queues in hospitals.
  • Concessions in medicines and travel.
  • State-specific pension schemes.

Reference: National Policy for Senior Citizens
