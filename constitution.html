Constitutional & Citizen Rights

Simple explanations of fundamental rights, equality, freedom of speech, and RTI.

Right to Information (RTI)

Citizens can seek information from public authorities.

  • File RTI online or offline with PIO.
  • Response within 30 days.
  • Appeal to First Appellate Authority if denied.

Reference: Right to Information Act, 2005

Freedom of Speech

Article 19(1)(a) guarantees free expression.

  • Subject to reasonable restrictions.
  • Includes right to protest peacefully.
  • Defamation or hate speech not protected.

Reference: Article 19, Constitution of India

Right to Equality

No discrimination on grounds of religion, caste, etc.

  • Equal protection of laws.
  • Reservation for backward classes.
  • Approach courts for violation.

Reference: Articles 14-16, Constitution
