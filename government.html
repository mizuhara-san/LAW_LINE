Government & Elections

Simple explanations of government schemes, voting rights, and grievance redressal.

How to File Grievance?

Against government departments.

  • Use CPGRAMS portal online.
  • Track status with reference number.
  • Escalate to higher authorities if unresolved.

Reference: Centralized Public Grievance Redress

Voter Rights

Every citizen above 18 can vote.

  • Register on voters.eci.gov.in.
  • Right to secret ballot.
  • Can challenge bogus voters.

Reference: Representation of the People Act
